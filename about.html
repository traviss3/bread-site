About 4218 Sourdough

4218 Sourdough was born out of downtime and total amazement with all things bread. I had the idea for this business back in 2021 shortly after my wife moved in with me. I love just being in the kitchen. There is nothing better than that yeasty smell soaking your house after a loaf, bagel, or bun comes hot out of the oven. So this my little way of trying to bring a piece of that to everyone
